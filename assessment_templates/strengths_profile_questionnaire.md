# Instructions
instructions: |
  For each of the 60 strengths below, rate yourself on THREE dimensions:
  
  1. **Performance** (1-5): How well do you perform this skill?
     - 1 = I struggle with this
     - 3 = I'm competent
     - 5 = I excel at this
  
  2. **Energy** (1-5): How does using this skill make you feel?
     - 1 = Drains me significantly
     - 3 = Neutral
     - 5 = Energizes me greatly
  
  3. **Frequency** (1-5): How often do you use this skill?
     - 1 = Rarely/Never
     - 3 = Sometimes
     - 5 = Daily/Constantly

  ## Quadrant Placement Logic
  After completing ratings, strengths are categorized:
  
  | Performance | Energy | Frequency | Quadrant |
  |-------------|--------|-----------|----------|
  | High (4-5) | High (4-5) | High (4-5) | **Realised Strength** |
  | High (4-5) | High (4-5) | Low (1-3) | **Unrealised Strength** |
  | High (4-5) | Low (1-3) | Variable | **Learned Behaviour** |
  | Low (1-3) | Low (1-3) | Variable | **Weakness** |
