# Values Alignment Questionnaire

Based on Brené Brown's "Living Into Our Values" framework from *Dare to Lead* (pages 185-197).

---

## Overview

Values are beliefs that are most important to you—they help you find your way in the dark and fill you with a feeling of purpose. This exercise helps you clarify your core values and translate them from abstract concepts into concrete behaviors.

---

## Step 1: Select Your Core Values

Review the list below and identify your **two to three** core values.

**When selecting your values, ask yourself:**
- Does this define me?
- Is this who I am at my best?
- Is this a filter that I use to make hard decisions?

## Scoring & Interpretation

This is not a scored assessment. The value lies in:
- **Clarity**: Knowing exactly what matters most to you
- **Specificity**: Translating abstract values into observable behaviors
- **Accountability**: Creating a system to notice when you're off track
- **Support**: Identifying people and practices that keep you aligned

---

## Retake Frequency

**Quarterly review recommended** to:
- Check if values still resonate
- Update supporting/slippery behaviors based on new insights
- Refresh your early warning indicators
