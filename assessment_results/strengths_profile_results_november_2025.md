## KEY INSIGHTS & PATTERNS

### Signature Strengths (Top 5 Realised)

1. **Curiosity** - Your #1 superpower. You are interested in everything and love to ask questions. This drives your deep industry knowledge and continuous learning.

2. **Narrator** - Stories come naturally. This supports your ability to convey insights, build relationships with stakeholders, and make complex ideas memorable.

3. **Gratitude** - You naturally appreciate the good things. This contributes to your warmth and positive presence in professional settings.

4. **Innovation** - You think out of the box and look beyond what exists. This drives your systems-building and process improvement work.

5. **Growth** - You actively seek opportunities to develop. This fuels your current focus on upskilling across product sense, finance, and communication.

### Highest Potential (Top 3 Unrealised)

1. **Resilience** - You have the ability to overcome adversity but don't use it as often. This is particularly relevant given your ADHD journey and career transition.

2. **Adherence** - You can follow processes well but may underutilize this. Could support your SOP-building and implementation work.

3. **Pride** - You take pride in quality work but may not leverage this consistently. Relevant for building your professional brand.

### Watch Zone (Top 3 Learned Behaviours)

⚠️ **Prevention** - You've learned to anticipate problems, but it may drain you. Watch for over-indexing on risk anticipation.

⚠️ **Work Ethic** - You've learned to work hard, but it's not energizing. Be mindful of burnout patterns.

⚠️ **Persistence** - You can keep going through difficulty, but it costs energy. Know when to pivot vs. persist.

### Development Areas (Top 3 Weaknesses)

🔴 **Personal Responsibility** - Following through on commitments is a challenge area. Use external scaffolding and accountability systems.

🔴 **Empathic** - Natural emotional connection with others doesn't come easily. Lean on your Connector and Explainer strengths instead.

🔴 **Emotional Awareness** - Reading others' emotions is difficult. Compensate by asking more questions and taking more time in interactions.
