# Chrononaut Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 12, 2025

## Overview

Chrononaut is a personal productivity application designed for individual use. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect

### From Whoop
- Recovery scores and heart rate variability
- Sleep duration and consistency metrics
- Daily strain scores
- Workout activities (type, duration, heart rate zones)
- Meditation sessions

### From Google Calendar
- Calendar event titles, times, and attendees
- Meeting links

### From TickTick
- Task titles, due dates, and completion status

### User-Generated
- Journal entries
- Notes and meeting notes
- Assessment responses

## How We Use Your Data

All data is used solely to:
- Display your personal metrics and calendar
- Provide AI-powered pattern analysis and coaching
- Generate personalized productivity insights

## Data Storage & Security

- Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security
- Sensitive content (journal entries, meeting notes) is encrypted client-side using AES-256-GCM
- OAuth tokens are stored encrypted
- All data transmission uses TLS 1.3

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to any third parties.**

Data is only shared with:
- **AI providers (Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini):** For pattern analysis and coaching features. These providers have no-training policies and data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.

## Data Retention

- Journal entries: Indefinite (user-controlled)
- Health metrics: 2 years
- Meeting transcripts: 90 days
- Calendar events: 30 days rolling window

## Your Rights

You can:
- Export your data at any time
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Disconnect any integration to stop data sync

## Single-User Application

Chrononaut is currently a personal application built and used by a single individual. There is no multi-user functionality or data sharing between users.
